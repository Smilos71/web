Step 1: Verify and Create Your Trigger

To set up a new automatic reply, you must submit your trigger for manual moderation. This ensures everything runs smoothly.

Send the verification email to the dedicated administrator address:

admin.sendbot@smilosak.rf.gd

TO: admin.sendbot@smilosak.rf.gd
SUBJECT: [YOUR TRIGGER KEYWORD, e.g., PRICE LIST]

BODY: [THE FULL MESSAGE YOU WANT SEND BOT TO SEND AUTOMATICALLY]

--- Example ---
**TO:** admin.sendbot@smilosak.rf.gd
**SUBJECT:** CATALOG
**BODY:** Hi %{FROM}, thanks for requesting our catalog! You can download the latest version here: [Link to your file]. Let us know if you have any questions. [Your Footer]

You will receive a confirmation email once your trigger content is reviewed and approved.

Step 2: Operational Use

Once your trigger is approved, instruct your customers to send their requests to your active SendBot address:

sendbot@smilosak.rf.gd

SendBot will then automatically monitor this inbox and reply based on your approved triggers.
