How to Get Access to the #ads Channel

To post a message in the #ads channel, you need to follow these steps:

  • It costs 200 SmilosCoins to send one advertisement message.
  • To purchase access, use: /item-buy #ads
  • To activate your access, use: /item use #ads

After activation, you will have 1 hour to post your message in the #ads channel.

Once you post, a slowmode will be activated, preventing further posts until the 1 hour expires.

If you wish to post again, repeat the process once the slowmode expires. Trying before it finishes won't work!
