Server Rules


Make sure to follow these rules to keep the community friendly and welcoming:

  1. Be Respectful: Treat others with respect. No sexist, offensive, or hurtful behavior.
  2. No Spam or Advertising: Do not share unsolicited links or advertisements.
  3. No Inappropriate Content: No offensive, racist, sexist, pornographic, or illegal content.
  4. Privacy: Don't share personal information without consent.
  5. Use the Right Channels: Post in channels that match the topic.
  6. Respect Moderators: Follow the decisions of moderators and admins.
  7. Languages: The main languages on the server are Czech, Slovak, and English.
  8. Age Limit: This server is for users 13+ (due to Discord's Terms of Service).
